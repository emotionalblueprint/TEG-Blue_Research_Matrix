### **1. Core Principles**

Every contribution is guided by three simple principles:

1. **Emotional safety before productivity**  
   We prioritize care, clarity, and respect before deadlines or output.  
   Emotional safety creates scientific integrity.

2. **Transparency over hierarchy**  
   Every voice is equal in the pursuit of truth.  
   We name what we see, question what we inherit, and stay accountable to what we create.

3. **Repair over perfection**  
   Mistakes are part of the process. What matters is how we repair and grow through them.

### **3. Contributing New Work**

You can contribute in three main ways:

1. **Add a new research line**  
   Expand the `TEG-Blue_Research_Matrix.csv` with your proposed study, using the same column format.  
   Include references and frameworks connected to your hypothesis.

2. **Add or adapt a tool**  
   Update the `TEG-Blue_Tool_Library.csv` with new or modified instruments, scales, or visual frameworks.  
   Include validation status and intended context of use.

3. **Propose documentation or analysis**  
   Add supporting Markdown files to `/docs/` with new insights, validation reports, or interpretive notes.

Before submitting a pull request, please ensure your contribution:
- Honors the emotional and ethical foundation of TEG-Blue.  
- Avoids using the material for profit, manipulation, or diagnostic claims.  
- Uses **plain, accessible language** â€” emotional clarity is part of scientific rigor.
